# Disconnect Wallet

To disconnect your app from the user's wallet, use the <code>wallet_renouncePermissions</code> method. This revokes all permissions previously granted by the user.

<CodeBlock code={disconnectCode} lang="typescript" title="Disconnect" />

<Callout type="info">
  After disconnecting, your app will need to request a new connection via
  <code>wallet_connect</code> before making any further requests.
</Callout>

## How it works

Calling <code>Wallet.disconnect()</code> internally calls <code>wallet_renouncePermissions</code> and clears the stored provider ID. This ensures a clean disconnection.

Users can also disconnect directly from their wallet UI. Your app should listen for the <code>disconnect</code> event to handle this case gracefully:
