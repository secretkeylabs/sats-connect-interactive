<H1>Disconnect Wallet</H1>

<P>
  To disconnect your app from the user's wallet, use the{" "}
  <code>wallet_renouncePermissions</code> method. This revokes all permissions
  previously granted by the user.
</P>

<CodeBlock code={disconnectCode} lang="typescript" title="Disconnect" />

<Callout type="info">
  After disconnecting, your app will need to request a new connection via{" "}
  <code>wallet_connect</code> before making any further requests.
</Callout>

<H2>How it works</H2>

<P>
  Calling <code>Wallet.disconnect()</code> internally calls{" "}
  <code>wallet_renouncePermissions</code> and clears the stored provider ID.
  This ensures a clean disconnection.
</P>

<P>
  Users can also disconnect directly from their wallet UI. Your app should
  listen for the <code>disconnect</code> event to handle this case gracefully:
</P>
